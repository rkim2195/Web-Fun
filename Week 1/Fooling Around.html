Some of the greatest running backs in the NFL

Here are some of the people who I think have been one of the greatest running backs in the league just last year.

  • Marshawn Lynch is one of the greatest running backs of all time, with a nickname like "Beast Mode" it is hard not to be thought of as a great running back.
  • Saquon Barkley the super rookie from the New York Giants has had a phenomal year. His physical prowess and dynamic vision has led to be one of the league's top running backs in his first year of playing in the NFL.
  • Kareem Hunt although has had some off field issues, thus resulting in a suspension from the NFL for approximately a year, he was a the running back who led the league in yards before his suspension.
